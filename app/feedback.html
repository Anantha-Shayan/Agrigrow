Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the soil information feature?

expand_more

To use the soil information feature, navigate to the 'Soil Health' section from the main menu. You can then either use your device's GPS to get data for your current location or manually search for a specific area. The app will provide detailed information on soil type, composition, and nutrient levels.

What are the best practices for pest detection?

expand_more

For effective pest detection, regularly inspect your crops, especially the undersides of leaves. Use the app's 'Pest ID' feature by taking a clear photo of the suspected pest or crop damage. The app will analyze the image and provide identification and treatment recommendations. Setting up monitoring traps can also provide early warnings.

How can I get personalized crop advice?

expand_more

Personalized crop advice is available in the 'My Farm' section. Complete your farm profile with details like crop types, planting dates, and farm size. Based on this information and local weather data, the app will generate tailored recommendations for irrigation, fertilization, and harvesting schedules to optimize your yield.
